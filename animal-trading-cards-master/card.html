Tiger

Tigers are stalk and ambush hunters; they lie in wait slowly creeping towards their prey until they are close enough to pounce.

  • Lifespan: 10 - 15 years
  • Bite power: 1,050 psi
  • Weight: 200 - 680 Ibs
  • Commonly found: Asia

A Tiger is a powerful carnivore, a hunter with sharp teeth, strong jaws, and a very agile body. They are the largest naturally occurring species of cat, of which the Siberian tiger is the largest, weighing up to 800 pounds and measuring up to 11 feet
head to tail.
